# Career Podcast Brand Foundation

> Version 0.1 --- Initial brand elements for market validation

This document intentionally defines only the visual foundation (color
palette and typography). Additional brand elements such as logo,
imagery, tone of voice, and messaging will be developed after the market
validation experiment.

## Brand Attributes

-   Curious
-   Human
-   Intelligent
-   Honest
-   Hopeful

## Color Palette

### Deep Navy

-   Hex: `#1E2A38`
-   Primary brand color for navigation, headings, buttons, and footer.

### Warm Ivory

-   Hex: `#F7F4EE`
-   Primary page background.

### Golden Ochre

-   Hex: `#D39B2A`
-   Accent color for CTAs, links, and highlights.

### Forest Green

-   Hex: `#496A4B`
-   Supporting accent for success states and secondary graphics.

### Slate Gray

-   Hex: `#5D6672`
-   Primary body text.

### Light Gray

-   Hex: `#E5E7EB`
-   Borders, cards, dividers, and subtle surfaces.

## Typography

### Headlines

**Fraunces**

Use for: - H1--H3 - Hero headlines - Feature callouts - Pull quotes

Characteristics: - Editorial - Warm - Intelligent - Memorable

### Body

**Inter**

Use for: - Body text - Navigation - Forms - Buttons - Captions -
Dashboard UI

Characteristics: - Highly readable - Modern - Neutral - Excellent for
digital interfaces

## Suggested Type Scale

  Element      Font
  ------------ ----------
  H1           Fraunces
  H2           Fraunces
  H3           Fraunces
  Body         Inter
  Navigation   Inter
  Forms        Inter
  Buttons      Inter
  Captions     Inter

## Guiding Principle

The visual identity should communicate discovery, curiosity, and
humanity rather than corporate professionalism. It should feel like an
invitation to explore new worlds of work.
